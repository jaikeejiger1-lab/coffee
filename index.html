OUR STORY

From farm to cup,
with love and intention

At Aurelia, we believe coffee is more than a beverage — it is a bridge between people and place. Our journey begins with direct relationships with smallholder farmers practicing regenerative agriculture across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Kenya.

Our master roasters, each with over twenty years of experience, treat every bean with the reverence it deserves. We roast in small batches using a vintage 1948 German Probat to preserve the unique character of each origin.

Every cup tells a complete farm-to-cup story: who grew it, how it was processed, and the exact day it was roasted. This transparency is our promise to you.
